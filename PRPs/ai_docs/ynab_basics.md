# YNAB Budgeting Fundamentals

This document summarizes core concepts from the You Need A Budget (YNAB) methodology, focusing on features relevant for a minimal budgeting application.

## Four Rules of YNAB

1. **Give Every Dollar a Job** – Assign each incoming dollar to a specific purpose before it is spent.
2. **Embrace Your True Expenses** – Break large or infrequent expenses into manageable monthly amounts.
3. **Roll With the Punches** – Adjust budgets when overspending occurs by moving money between categories.
4. **Age Your Money** – Aim to use money that is at least 30 days old, creating a buffer between income and spending.

## Core Entities

- **Accounts** – Track balances for checking, savings, or cash.
- **Categories** – Buckets that group planned spending (e.g., Rent, Groceries).
- **Transactions** – Income or expense entries linked to an account and category.
- **Budgets** – Allocations of available funds across categories.
